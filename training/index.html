Graduate students

Prospective graduate students are encouraged to contact Dr. Wang and apply to either the Microbiology Program or the Genome, Cell, and Developmental Biology (GCDB) Program at Indiana University Bloomington. Students already enrolled in one of these programs should reach out to both their program director and Dr. Wang to discuss lab rotation opportunities.

Postdoctoral fellows

We are currently accepting applications for a postdoctoral fellow position. We are especially interested in candidates with strong enthusiasm for bacterial cell and chromosome biology, and experience in genetics, biochemistry, molecular biology, or cell biology. To apply, please email Dr. Wang with a brief message describing your research interests, your motivation for joining the lab, and why you believe you would be a good fit.
